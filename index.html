Welcome to Yage.se

At Yage.se, we honor the ancient healing traditions rooted in Amazonian shamanism and the profound transformation that plant medicines can offer when guided with wisdom and care. Our mission is to connect people in Sweden with experienced shamans who walk the path of ancestral knowledge and healing.

This website is your hub for learning more about sacred plant medicine and the retreats we organize in Sweden. These gatherings are not on a fixed schedule — they unfold in harmony with the availability of the shamans who travel from distant lands to share their work. Most retreats take place around the Stockholm area.

To stay informed about upcoming retreats, we invite you to subscribe to our newsletter below. It's the primary way we share news and updates.

We recognize and respect the many healing modalities around the world. Amazonian shamanism has a rightful place beside both Western and Eastern traditions. The shamans we work with have dedicated years to their medicine path and bring with them deep experience in guiding others. We believe that true healing flourishes in a space of safety, clarity, and intention — and that’s what we strive to create with each retreat.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is plant medicine and how is it used in your retreats?

Plant medicine refers to sacred preparations made from medicinal plants from the Amazon, traditionally used for healing, self-discovery, and spiritual growth. In our retreats, Amazonian shamans guide participants through ceremonial experiences that include intention setting, and deep inner work, held in a safe and sacred space.

Is it safe?

Yes. Our retreats are guided by trained shamans with years of experience and include participant health screening. Get in touch if you have questions specific to your health history.

Do I need experience to join?

No. Beginners and experienced participants are welcome. We provide full support before and after the ceremony.

Where are the retreats held?

Most retreats take place in nature at different locations around Sweden.

How should I prepare?

Preparation includes both mental and physical aspects. We recommend a dieta — a period of intentional eating and lifestyle adjustments — for at least one week before the retreat. Avoid alcohol, caffeine, processed foods, pork, and sexual activity. Prioritize rest, hydration, simple plant-based meals, and time in nature. Set clear intentions and come with an open heart and mind.

How do I sign up?

Subscribe to our newsletter at the bottom of the page to be notified when a new retreat is announced.

FOR WOMEN: Can I attend during menstruation?

In some Amazonian traditions, menstruation is considered a sacred and energetically sensitive time. As a result, certain shamans may prefer that women do not participate in ceremonies during their menstrual cycle. Others are open to participation depending on the context and intention. We ask that you let us know in advance, and we will clarify based on the guidance of the shaman leading the retreat.
